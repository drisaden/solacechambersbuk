who we are

Committed to improving the standard and competence of our members while providing doors for mentorships and opportunities. Opened to sponsorship and partnership.

Mission Statement

Our mission at Solace Chambers is to provide a dynamic and supportive environment where law students can develop their skills, knowledge, and character, empowering them for successful careers in the legal industry and leadership roles.

Our Vision

Our vision is to continually be a leading student-based Chambers, known for our commitment to building competence, excellence, integrity, and professionalism. We envision a future where members, as aspirants to the bar, leverage mentorship for a fulfilling legal career.

Our Objectives

1. Foster Excellence: Provide a supportive environment that nurtures legal excellence, innovation, and advocacy among law students.

2. Develop Skills: Equip members with comprehensive and up-to-date knowledge, legal skills, and best practices required for success in the legal profession.

3. Promote Professional Development: Offer training, mentorship, and networking opportunities to enhance members' career prospects and professional growth.

OUR DIRECTORATES AND Committees

Behind the functionality of Solace Chambers are our standing Directorates comprising of commiitted, tireless members.

Research and Litigation Directorate

At the heart of its operation is the Directorate of Research and Litigation, which drives the Chambers' intellectual and advocacy pursuits. The Directorate ensures continuous learning and a positive impact on our members with a quest to raise the bar.

The Directorate organizes and facilitates a series of insightful programs and events, spanning across different aspects of law with the aim of equipping members with the knowledge essential for sound legal reasoning. The Directorate also focuses on advocacy training, procedural law, and trial techniques, preparing respected partners for real-world legal practice.

The Directorate occupies a central position in the Chambers structure, fostering a culture of excellence, student legal training, and professional readiness. At the helm of the Directorate lies the Director of Research and Dispute Resolution, the two Assistants, and a number of committed and respected members as members of the Directorate.

The Directorate specifically engage in activities including:

  • • Moot and Mock Competition
  • • Weekly Legal Bit Publication
  • • Procedural Quiz Competition
  • • Weekly Advocacy Clases

Media and Publicity Directorate

At the heart of the Chambers' communication strategy is the Media and Publicity Directorate, driving the organization's brand visibility and public image. The Directorate ensures consistent and engaging communication, fostering a culture of transparency, creativity, and excellence.

The Directorate organizes and facilitates various media and publicity initiatives, spanning across publications, video content creation, social media management, and magazine production. With the aim of showcasing the Chambers' activities, achievements, and values, the Directorate prepares compelling content that educates, entertains, and inspires.

The Media and Publicity Directorate forms a vital part of the Chambers, cultivating a spirit of creativity, innovation, and communication excellence. At the helm of the Directorate lies the Director of Media and Publicity, supported by dedicated members who share a passion for storytelling and brand promotion.

The Directorate specifically engage in functions including:

  • • Publishings and circulating the Chambers press releases.
  • • Creating hilarious, educating, and entertaining video content.
  • • Managing and maintaining the Chambers' social media handles.
  • • Designing, editing, and producing the annual magazine.
  • • Developing and implementing comprehensive communication strategies. - Collaborating with other directorates to promote events and activities.
  • • Maintaining the Chambers' website and online presence.

Operations and Technical Committee

At the core of the Chambers' technical operations is the Operations & Technical Committee, driving the organization's technical infrastructure and operational efficiency. The Committee ensures seamless technical support and maintenance, fostering a culture of innovation and excellence.

The Committee organizes and facilitates various technical tasks, spanning across website management, graphics design, Google Forms creation, and other operational needs. With the aim of equipping the Chambers with efficient technical solutions, the Committee prepares members for real-world applications and technical challenges.

The Operations & Technical Committee forms a vital part of the Chambers, cultivating a spirit of technical expertise, innovation, and collaboration. At the helm of the Committee lies the Director of Operations and Technical, supported by dedicated members who share a passion for technical excellence.

The Committee specifically engage in functions including:

  • • Creation of Google Forms for various purposes.
  • • Graphics design for the Chambers.
  • • Solace website overall management.
  • • Other technical and operational tasks as needed.

Academic Committee

At the forefront of academic excellence is the Academic Committee, driving the Chambers' educational pursuits and supporting members' academic growth. The Committee ensures continuous learning, academic guidance, and counseling, fostering a culture of intellectual curiosity and excellence.

The Committee organizes and facilitates various academic programs and events, spanning across tutorials, academic guidance, and counseling. With the aim of equipping members with essential knowledge and skills, the Committee prepares members for academic success and professional excellence.

The Academic Committee forms a vital part of the Chambers, cultivating a spirit of academic rigor, innovation, and support. At the helm of the Committee lies the Director of Academic Affairs, supported by dedicated members who share a passion for academic excellence.

The Committee specifically engage in activities including:

  • • Organizing tutorials for members.
  • • Providing academic guidance and counseling by hosting Webinars on various academic topics.
  • • Partnering with other faculty students' associations, such as LAWSAN BUK.

Poetry and Debate Committee

At the forefront of creative expression and argumentative excellence is the Poetry and Debate Committee, driving the Chambers' artistic and rhetorical pursuits. The Committee ensures continuous creative growth and a positive impact among members, fostering a culture of eloquence, innovation, and intellectual exploration.

The Committee organizes and facilitates a range of engaging programs and events, spanning across various aspects of poetry, debate, and public speaking. With the aim of equipping members with essential skills in creative writing, critical thinking, and persuasive argumentation, the Committee prepares members for real-world applications and competitions.

The Poetry and Debate Committee forms a vital part of the Chambers, cultivating a spirit of creativity, intellectual curiosity, and effective communication. At the helm of the Committee lies the Director of Poetry and Debate, supported by dedicated members who share a passion for artistic expression and argumentative excellence.

The Committee specifically engage in functions including:

  • • Weekly Poetry Lines Publication
  • • Organising Debate Competitions for members
  • • Facilitating the annual poetry review contest
